Marijuana in Greece

Independent informational guide explaining cannabis laws, medical use, and safety considerations for visitors to Athens and Thessaloniki.

Last updated: 2025 • Laws can change — always check official sources

Is Marijuana Legal in Greece?

Recreational marijuana is illegal in Greece.

Cannabis containing THC is prohibited under Greek law throughout the country, including Athens and Thessaloniki. There are no legal recreational dispensaries, clubs, or retail stores.

Possession, use, or sale may result in fines, court proceedings, or imprisonment, depending on the amount and circumstances.

Medical Cannabis & CBD

Medical cannabis is legal only with a prescription from a Greek-licensed physician and must be obtained through approved pharmacies.

Foreign medical prescriptions are generally not accepted.

CBD products derived from industrial hemp with very low THC are legal and widely available, but they do not produce a psychoactive effect.

Tourist Information: Nightlife & Cannabis Rumors

Visitors often hear claims that marijuana is easy to find around nightlife, bars, or clubs. These claims usually come from online forums, social media, or word-of-mouth rather than official sources.

There are NO legal bars, clubs, or venues in Greece that sell marijuana.

Any cannabis offered in nightlife areas is illegal and unregulated. Tourists are fully subject to Greek law.

Dangers & Risks for Tourists

Important: Visitors should understand the risks before relying on informal advice.
  • Legal consequences: Enforcement exists, even for small amounts.
  • Detention or fines: Legal issues can escalate quickly.
  • Scams: Visitors are frequently overcharged or misled.
  • Health risks: Unknown quality and no safety controls.
  • Travel impact: Arrests can affect visas and future entry.

Athens

Athens actively enforces national drug laws. Public possession or use can result in fines or arrest.

Any recreational cannabis found in the city originates from illegal sources.

Thessaloniki

The same national laws apply in Thessaloniki. Recreational marijuana remains illegal.

There are no legal ways to purchase THC cannabis outside the medical system.

Black Market & Risks

Cannabis sold outside the medical system is illegal and unregulated.

Unregulated sources carry legal, financial, and personal safety risks, particularly for tourists unfamiliar with local laws.
